<span data-ttu-id="587fb-106">健康保險流通與責任法案 (HIPAA) 是一種美國保健法律，可建立用於個別身分識別健康資訊的使用、披露和保護的需求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="587fb-106">The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) is a US healthcare law that establishes requirements for the use, disclosure, and safeguarding of individually identifiable health information.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="587fb-107">它適用于涵蓋的實體、醫生的辦公室、醫院、醫療保險公司和其他保健公司，可讓您存取患者的受保護健康情況資訊 (PHI) 以及商務夥伴（例如雲端服務和 IT 提供者），以代表自行處理 PHI。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="587fb-107">It applies to covered entities, doctors' offices, hospitals, health insurers, and other healthcare companies, with access to patients' protected health information (PHI), as well as to business associates, such as cloud service and IT providers, that process PHI on their behalf.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="587fb-108"> (大多數涵蓋的實體不會自行執行宣告或資料處理等功能。他們會依靠業務相關的方式來執行。 ) </span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="587fb-108">(Most covered entities do not carry out functions such as claims or data processing on their own; they rely on business associates to do so.)</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="587fb-114">HIPAA 的範圍已擴充，其經濟和臨床健康情況 (高科技) 法案的狀況資訊技術的 enactment。一起，HIPAA 和高科技法案規則包括：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="587fb-114">The scope of HIPAA was extended with the enactment of the Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health (HITECH) Act. - <span data-ttu-id="587fb-116">HIPAA 安全性規則，它會設定系統管理、技術和實體保護的標準，以保護電子 PHI，避免未經授權的存取、使用和披露。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="587fb-116">The HIPAA Security Rule, which sets the standards for administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect electronic PHI from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="587fb-117">它還包含組織的需求，例如業務關聯協定 (Baa) 。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="587fb-117">It also includes such organizational requirements as Business Associate Agreements (BAAs).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="587fb-118">高科技違犯通知的最後規則，需要在遭到不安全的 PHI 遭到破壞時，對個人和政府進行通知。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="587fb-118">The HITECH Breach Notification Final Rule, which requires giving notice to individuals and the government when a breach of unsecured PHI occurs.</span></span>
