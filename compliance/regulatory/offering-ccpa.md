## <a name="ccpa-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="2978c-105">CCPA 概述</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2978c-105">CCPA overview</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2978c-106">加州消費者隱私權法案 (CCPA) 是美國的第一個綜合隱私權法律。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2978c-106">The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) is the first comprehensive privacy law in the United States.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2978c-107">它為加州消費者提供各種隱私權權利。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2978c-107">It provides a variety of privacy rights to California consumers.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="2978c-108">由 CCPA 管制的企業將會對這些消費者負有許多義務，包括披露、一般資料保護法規 (GDPR) 類似的使用者資料主體權力 (Dsr) 、「自願檢出」（適用于特定資料傳輸）和「選擇性加入」對未成年人的需求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2978c-108">Businesses regulated by the CCPA will have a number of obligations to those consumers, including disclosures, General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)-like consumer data subject rights (DSRs), an 'opt-out' for certain data transfers, and an 'opt-in' requirement for minors.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2978c-109">CCPA 只適用于經營于加州公司的公司，其可滿足下列一或多項功能： (1) 具有超過 $25000000 的年收入，或 (2) 從加州消費者個人資訊的銷售中取得年收入超過50% 的年收入，或 (3) 購買，並每年為超過超過50000的加州消費者購買或分享個人資訊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2978c-109">The CCPA only applies to companies doing business in California which satisfy one or more of the following: (1) have a gross annual revenue of more than $25 million, or (2) derive more than 50% of their annual income from the sale of California consumer personal information, or (3) buy, sell or share the personal information of more than 50,000 California consumers annually.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2978c-110">CCPA 會在 2020 年 1 月 1 日生效。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2978c-110">The CCPA goes into effect on January 1, 2020.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2978c-111">不過，加州律師一般 (AG) 會從2020年7月1日開始執行。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2978c-111">However, enforcement by the California Attorney General (AG) will start on July 1, 2020.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2978c-112">加州 AG 會強制執行 CCPA，且會有電來發出非規範罰款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2978c-112">The California AG will enforce the CCPA and will have power to issue non-compliance fines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2978c-113">CCPA 也會提供動作的私人權利，該權利限制于資料違規。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2978c-113">The CCPA also provides a private right of action which is limited to data breaches.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2978c-114">根據私人訴訟權，每位消費者每起事件造成的損失在 100 美元到 750 美元之間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2978c-114">Under the private right of action, damages can come in between $100 and $750 per incident per consumer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2978c-115">加州檢察總長也可以全面執行 CCPA，並能夠對每次違規行為處以 2500 美元以下，或對每次故意違規行為處以 7500 美元以下的民事罰鍰。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2978c-115">The California AG also can enforce the CCPA in its entirety with the ability to levy a civil penalty of not more than $2,500 per violation or $7,500 per intentional violation.</span></span>
